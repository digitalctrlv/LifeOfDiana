Idea

Diana Frances Spencer, or most commonly known as Princess Diana was a born on July 1, 1961 at Sandringham, England. Diana grow up close to the royal family, this because the Spencer family is part of British nobility. In 1975 her father inherited the title of Earl Spencer, since then she became Lady Diana.

Diana first met Charles, (King Charles III, former Prince of Wales) back in 1977. Later on in 1980 Charles and Diana saw each other again and after a couple of months and Diana’s good impression to the Queen after a weekend in Balmoral Castel, Scotland. Charles proposed to Diana, the engagement was publicly announce on February 24, 1981 with a BBC Interview.

Just six months later they got married on July 29, 1981 at St’ Paul Cathedral, London. The wedding was watched by a global television audience of 750 million people. The service was described as a fairytale wedding, becoming one of the most important events of the 1980’s. After the wedding Diana became officially a member of the Royal family, therefore becoming Diana, Princess of Wales.

In their firs years of marriage she give birth to Prince William and Prince Harry, while caring out all the official engagements as a royal family member. She was particularly involved with several charities and went all around to support them. Is specially remembered for reducing stigma around HIV/AIDS during the mid 1980’s epidemic of the disease

During the 80’s an 90’s she became a fashion icon, a celebrity and a beloved woman all around the world, remembered for her sensibility and her kindness specially for her caring attitude towards the ones in need.

Unfortunately her popularity all around the world start to produce problems in her marriage with Prince Charles. In addition do to his extramarital affairs, their marriage started to crumble. By the beginning of the 1990’s the relationship between the Charles and Diana was in crisis, all the UK tabloids were every day fill with a new gossip.

After several scandals such as the publication of Diana biography, Diana and Charles separated in 1992. By 1996 Charles asked the Queen the permission to divorce and it was granted after the famous Panorama interview where Diana exposed directly Prince Charles and the royal family.

In the last year of her life she got closer and began a relationship with Dodi Al-Fayed an Egyptian millionaire, they spend the summer of 1997 together. In Paris, France on the night of August 31, after being chased by paparazzi and the driver of her car being under alcohol effects, Diana tragically died on a car crash in the Alma bridge.

A week after her death her state funeral was carried out in London, where over 2.5 billion people around the world mourn her death in TV.

After her death, Diana is remembered as one of the most influenced woman of the 20 century, deeply beloved by her charming attitude, kindness and amazing fashion sense. Even after more than 25 years of her death she is still honored with movies, TV series and charities on her name. Today she is still an icon for several generations and a source of inspiration, remembered as Diana, the People’s Princess.

A LODLAM project

Life of Diana is produced as an assigment for the course 'Knowledge Organization, Libraries and Archives' as part of the master Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the University of Bologna, Italy (academic year 2024/2025). The goal of the project was to "create structured data starting from an idea and related items, and to document the work in a web page, including all files produced." LODLAM stands for Linked Open Data Libraries Archives Museums and hence we have aimed to use as many relevant metadata standards and ontologies as possible to enhance the semantic power of our ontology.

Spencer Tiara

Tiara

Is a unique piece of jewelry of diamonds in silver settings mounted in gold in various floral shapes assembly by Garrard & Co as a tiara in 1937, own by the Spencer family. Used by Lady Diana during her wedding to Prince Charles, it was characteristic because she decided to wear a tiara from her family and not a jewel of the crown.

Learn More
Lady Diana Spencer's Wedding Dress

Wedding Dress

Is the dress worn by Lady Diana for her wedding to Prince Charles in 1981 at St’Paul Cathedral in London. Was an ivory silk taffeta and antique lace gown, with a 25-foot train and a 140 m tulle veil. Is considered today one of the most iconic wedding dresses in history, becoming inspiration for the 1980’s fashion.

Kensington Palace

Building

Kensington Palace is an XVIII century palace of the royal family that work as residence for several royal family members. It became the official residence of Princess Diana since the day after the wedding until her death.

Alma Bridge

Building

Pont de l’Alma is a road bridge that goes across the Seine in Paris, France. Was build in 1856 and takes its name from the victory of France during the battle of Alma in the Crimean War. Now a days is known for being the site of the car crash where Princess Diana died in 1997.
